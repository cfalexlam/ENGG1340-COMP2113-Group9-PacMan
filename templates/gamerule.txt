------------------------------------------------------------
                        Game rule
'=': wall  'X': Pacman  'Q': ghost  'O': pellet  '.': dot

1. Use arrow keys to control the Pacman to eat all dots!
2. You lose 1 life each time you are caught by the ghost
3. Eating a pellet allows you to catch the ghosts back 
   until your strength becomes 0
4. You lose the game if you lose all 3 lives
